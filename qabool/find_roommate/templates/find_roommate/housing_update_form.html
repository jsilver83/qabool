Peace, mercy and blessings of God..

We welcome you to this special site with the help of freshmen students eligible for university accommodation to search for a colleague adequate housing.

This site allows you to choose a fellow adequate housing, through the information that will appear to you and other colleagues are also looking for a fellow for housing.

Site work method:

  • The site gives you access to personal information, and additional all new students who are looking for a colleague for housing.
  • For more information you can communicate with those who are interested in housing with the private means of communication.
  • When agreeing with a colleague adequate housing, log back on this site, and choose not to question (Are you looking for a fellow housing? *) So that your information is automatically removed from the list of fellow seekers housing.
